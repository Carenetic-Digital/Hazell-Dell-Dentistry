# Project Intake

## Business Information

**Business Name:** Hazel Dell Dentistry

## Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Patients of all ages in and around Carmel, IN (Hazel Dell Pkwy area) seeking a full-service neighborhood dentist for general, cosmetic, and emergency dental care.
